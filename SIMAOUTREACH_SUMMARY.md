# SimaOutreach — AI Outreach Studio

## What It Is

SimaOutreach is an AI outreach studio that researches every prospect live on the web, writes a unique personalized cold email for each, scores it on 5 dimensions, auto-improves any email below 75/100, and delivers it ready to send — for $7 per 50 emails.

**Website:** simaoutreach.com  
**Domain:** simaoutreach.com  
**Tech Stack:** Next.js + Neon Postgres + Perplexity (research) + DeepSeek (writing) + Vercel  

---

## The Problem It Solves

Cold email tools (Instantly, Lemlist, Smartlead) charge $39-79/month for sending infrastructure. But the actual work — researching each prospect, writing unique emails, personalizing subject lines — still falls on you. That costs 5+ hours and $250+ in time for a 50-email campaign.

**Total real cost with traditional tools: $322 for 50 emails.**  
**Total cost with SimaOutreach: $7 for 50 emails.**

---

## How It Works (The 7-Step Production Flow)

1. **Upload** — CSV of prospects (name, email, company, title)
2. **Research** — AI searches the web live via Perplexity for each prospect: funding rounds, product launches, growth signals, press coverage
3. **Write** — DeepSeek writes a unique email per prospect using only verified facts. Not a template — each email is different
4. **Score** — Every email scored on 5 dimensions: personalization, spam risk, readability, CTA clarity, subject line
5. **Auto-Revise** — Below 75/100? AI automatically rewrites using scorer tips + research. User sees before/after score improvement
6. **Refine** — Conversational AI editor: "make it shorter", "add their funding round", "apply to all drafts"
7. **Learn** — AI tracks which hook types work across campaigns and recommends strategies for next campaign

## Personalization Principles

### What our AI researches:
- Company news from the last 12 months
- Funding rounds, acquisitions, product launches
- Growth signals — headcount, customer wins, market expansion
- Professional talks at named conferences
- Published articles and press quotes

### What our AI NEVER touches:
- Social media feeds (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram)
- Personal life, family, hobbies, lifestyle
- Location or education details
- Job change history
- Anything requiring scrolling someone's feed

### The Conference Test:
**If you wouldn't say it to someone you just met at a professional conference, we don't put it in the email.**

## Key Features

### Production Studio
- Real-time generation with per-contact progress tracking
- Status flow: Researching → Writing → Scoring → Revising → Done
- Research card showing what Perplexity found per contact
- Score badge, hook type pill, auto-revision indicator (58→82)
- Progress bar with live campaign stats

### Email Scoring (5 Dimensions)
| Dimension | Weight | What It Checks |
|---|---|---|
| Personalization | 30% | Company name, specific numbers, facts, product references |
| Spam Risk | 20% | Trigger words, caps, exclamation marks, links |
| Readability | 15% | Word count (50-150 ideal), sentence length, paragraphs |
| CTA Clarity | 20% | Has CTA, is it a question, is it specific |
| Subject Line | 15% | Length, personalization, spam words, uniqueness |

### Auto-Revision Engine
- If email scores < 75, DeepSeek auto-rewrites using scorer tips + research data
- Shows first draft score vs. final score (e.g., 58 → 82)
- Only uses research already fetched (no extra Perplexity calls)
- Costs ~$0.005 per revision

### Conversational AI Editor
- Type natural language instructions: "make it shorter", "remove the pitch"
- Works on single email or all drafts at once
- 30 refinements per campaign limit (abuse protection)
- Shows refinement count and limit reached warning

### Research Cards
- Collapsible card per email showing Perplexity findings
- Research quality indicator: Strong (4+ facts), Moderate (2-3), Limited (1), Minimal
- Fact detection: counts $amounts, percentages, dates, named entities
- Hook type badge: Funding, Product Launch, Growth, General

### Hook Intelligence
- Auto-classifies hook type per email: funding, product_launch, growth, person_quote, general
- Tracks performance across campaigns
- Recommends which hooks to use for next campaign
- Example: "Funding hooks: 14% reply rate. Generic: 3%"

### Smart Follow-ups
- Day 3: Different angle, no repeat of original pitch
- Day 7: Final touch — demo link or graceful close
- Engagement-aware: different follow-up if clicked vs. ignored vs. no activity
- Threaded correctly (In-Reply-To, References headers)
- Generated by DeepSeek (no extra Perplexity calls)

### Tracking
- **Click Tracking:** URL wrapping in plain text, redirect via /t/:sendId/:trackId
- **Open Tracking:** Optional per campaign (user chooses, tradeoff warning shown)
- **Reply Detection:** IMAP polling with AI classification (interested, not interested, OOO, question)

### Campaign Analytics
- Campaign health score (circular ring)
- Score distribution bar chart
- 5-dimension radar chart
- Stat cards (avg personalization, spam safety, readability, CTA)
- Top issues aggregation across all emails
- Hook type breakdown

### Security
- AES-256-GCM encryption for all PII at rest
- Encrypted: contact emails, names, email subjects/bodies, SMTP passwords
- SHA-256 hash for dedup (can't reverse to get email)
- User's own SMTP — their domain, their reputation
- Anti-creep rules hardcoded in AI prompts

## Architecture

```
Frontend: Next.js (App Router) + Tailwind + Recharts
Database: Neon Postgres (serverless)
AI Research: Perplexity API (sonar model)
AI Writing: DeepSeek API (deepseek-chat model)
Email Sending: Nodemailer (user's SMTP)
Encryption: AES-256-GCM (Node.js crypto)
Scheduling: cron-job.org (free, every 30 min)
Hosting: Vercel (Hobby plan)
Auth: Google OAuth + JWT
Payments: Razorpay (when ready)
```

### Database Tables
- `users` — auth, plan, SMTP config (encrypted), usage tracking
- `campaigns` — name, mode, status, follow-up settings, refinement limits
- `contacts` — encrypted email/name, company, title, research data, email hash for dedup
- `email_sends` — encrypted subject/body, scores, hook type, status, threading
- `click_tracking` — per-link click tracking with IP/UA
- `excluded_emails` — opt-out list per user
- `payments` — Razorpay integration
